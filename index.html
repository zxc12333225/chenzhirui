My name is Chen Zhirui. I was born in Ji 'an city, Jiangxi Province in June 1999. I majored in Communication studies in School of Journalism, Communication University of China from 2017 to 2021. Now I am studying for a master's degree in Journalism (New Media Communication Research), School of Journalism and Communication, University of Chinese Academy of Social Sciences. I am a member of the Social Computing and Social Intelligence Committee of Chinese Artificial Intelligence Association. Member, International Society for Media and Communication Studies (2020-2021,2021-2022). Research interests: New media and social development, media convergence, data journalism, computing communication, etc. At present, I have published many CSSCI Journal papers and one English academic paper in Journal of Physics: Conference Series (EI included).
